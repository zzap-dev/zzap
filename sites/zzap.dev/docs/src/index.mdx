## In a nutshell

- zzap is **fast**. Build a complex website with thousands of pages in less than 300ms. No caching.
- zzap is **focused**. It has one goal, help you generate a website based on content. Wherever it comes from (disk, API, etc).
- zzap is **stable**. It relies on proven and stable technologies like Bun, JSX, and TailwindCSS.
- zzap is **seo-first**. It forces you to think about how your content is structured and how it is presented to search engines.
- zzap is **freeing**. Organize your code however you want. Give us our config, we'll do the rest.
